About Me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Chandlor Stauff, I am a current student at Boise State University. I am currently pursuing to get my degree in their GIMM program. The purpose of this website is to get to know a little about me and to share the work I have completed while being in GIMM.

The GIMM program, or Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile, is a program at Boise State which gives students the knowledge to use and manage modern technology for everyday uses. Before I entered the GIMM program, I knew nothing about the programs and softwares that were introduced. But now being in my second year, I have gained tremendous knowledge on the things we have learned. My experience since being in the GIMM program has been phenomenal. I have learned so much and I continue to learn new skills that I didn't know before.
